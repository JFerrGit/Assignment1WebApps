Dishes

Appetizers

Succulent, oriental delights made fresh to create the perfect start to a wholesome meal.

  • Egg Roll - Comes stuffed with pork, shrimp, or chicken.
  • Fried Dumplings - Set of (10) with choices of flour or wheat.
  • Spring Roll - Breaded dumpling with dim sum.
  • Shrimp Roll - Breaded with fresh shrimp caught locally.
  • Fried Wonton - Pork filling with duck sauce.

Soups

Gourmet blends of traditional herbs and spices with fresh broth and stock prepared hourly.

  • Wonton Soup - Minced pork and wonton noodles in simmering broth.
  • Egg Drop Soup - Thick and savory with fresh chicken broth.
  • Chicken Noodle Soup - Oriental classic with freshly roasted chicken.
  • Vegetable Soup - Corn, carrots, broccoli, and vegetable broth.
  • Seafood Soup - Crab and shrimp marinated in a seasoned saltwater broth.

Fried Rice

Locally grown and freshly dried rice fried and served with a side of soy sauce.

  • Vegetable Fried Rice - Rice fried with mixed house vegetables for a green flavor.
  • Chicken Fried Rice - Chicken flavor fried right into the rice for a wholesome poultry taste.
  • Roasted Pork Fried Rice - Roasted pork and rice for a classic savory flavor in every grain.
  • Beef Fried Rice - A more solid blend of beef and rice fried to taste.
  • Shrimp Fried Rice - Rice with the flavor of the sea thanks to savory shrimp.

Meats

Sustainable slaughter with local, fresh fish, beef, and chicken, all roasted to perfection.

  • Orange Chicken - Chicken cooked and breaded in our signature orange sauce.
  • Bejing Beef - Our beef counterpart to the classic orange chicken, with a zesty hint of spice.
  • Beef with Broccoli - A traditional combination of savory beef and seared broccoli.
  • Roasted Pork with Broccoli - Pork chops roasted with broccoli and seasoned, cooked tender.
  • Kung Pao Shrimp - Classic seafood dish with signature sichuan sauce and white pepper.
